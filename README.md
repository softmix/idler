# Void Harvest

An idle game where you extract energy from the quantum void. Single HTML file, no dependencies.

**[Play it](https://softmix.github.io/idler/)**

## Gameplay

- Click the animated orb (or press Space) to harvest energy
- Buy 7 tiers of generators for auto-income (Quantum Tap through Entropy Engine)
- Buy amplifiers to multiply click power (up to 32x)
- Generators unlock progressively as you buy into each tier
- Press 1-7 to quick-buy generators

## Visual features

- Canvas-drawn orb with swirling arcs, orbiting particles, and click burst ripples
- Orb intensifies as your income grows
- Particle explosions and floating damage numbers on click
- Progress bars on upgrade cards showing how close you are to affording them
