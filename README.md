# Idler

A collection of single-file idle games. **[Play them](https://softmix.github.io/idler/)**

## Games

### Void Harvest

Extract energy from the quantum void. Canvas-animated orb, 7 generator tiers, click amplifiers, milestones, offline progress, auto-save. [Play](void-harvest.html)

### Ore Drifter

Space mining idle game. Drill asteroids, collect rocks with a tractor beam, haul ore across a ring of stations and fields, and play the supply/demand market. 5 ore types, 6 asteroid fields, 5 stations, equipment upgrades, shield mechanics, and a refinery bonus system. [Play](ore-drifter.html)
